Web Design Agency in Costa Rica

Meseguer & Sucesores, S.A., through their websites www.mischunches.com and www.sitiosweb-costarica.com, is a creative web design agency, established in Heredia, Costa Rica since 2001, which specializes in the development of websites for small businesses and non-profit organizations.

There's a divide between the marketing available to large corporations and small businesses. Although there are many options for small businesses to choose from, none of them ever really feel like they're designed to fit your business

We're changing the game up, by giving 'small businesses' the opportunity to have the tools they need to grow and utilize the Internet without the burden of overpriced services designed to target their bigger competitors.

Our Mission

To enhancing the business growth of our customers with Creative Web Design that creates value and consistent competitive advantage for our clients.

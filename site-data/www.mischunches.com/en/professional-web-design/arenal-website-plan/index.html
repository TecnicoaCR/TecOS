Arenal Plan

This plan is for a website of (10) pages, ideal for medium to large companies, Asociaciones Solidaristas and Organizations wishing to offer their products and / or services in the Internet.

A common layout for this plan is:

  1. Home Page
  2. About Us (Company) Page
  3. Products and services (several pages)
  4. Customers or Gallery
  5. Contact Page

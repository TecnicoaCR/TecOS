OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Skycam International will strive to distribute the highest quality products at the most competitive pricing on the market. Customers needs will be satisfied in the upmost quality manner appropriate. Skycam International will seek to acquire mutually rewarding relationships with its customers, partner, suppliers and employees. Most importantly, all our activities will be carried out to the highest professional and ethical criterion.

OUR HISTORY

Skycam International was founded in 2015 and it is made up of engineers and representatives who sincerely want to help and provide the best support possible when is needed the most and with the most precise timing to all our clients. Also, all our representatives have the industry background to help our clients make the proper decision regarding the best technology in the world today. Skycam International is further made up of past integrators and distributors who are willing to provide a one complete solution to the end users as well as the integrators who are in need of major support.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

Our difference in comparison to the competition is that another of our focus primarily begins with the client’s familiarization of the well-known brands in the markets; in order to provide them a general product awareness and thus expediting their decision making regarding all of their projects requirements.

WHAT WE DO

Suppliers serve a huge range of customers on behalf of vendors. These customers range in size from small, independent resellers to large multinational retailers, but all have the common requirement of a “one-stop shop,” where they can source products and services from the hundreds of suppliers whose products are part of their own offering to end customers.

Our difference in comparison to the competition is that another of our focus primarily begins with the client’s familiarization of the well-known brands in the market such as Axis, Bosch, Dell, Samsung, Pelco, Pivot3, among many others in order to provide them a general product awareness and thus expediting their decision making regarding all of their projects requirements.

Our Focus

Our focus is to create product awareness, to provide quick technical support and also to give our clients a better understanding of what the best solution will be for their current project needs. We invest all the time necessary to understand the current technology and the new technology which is currently been created by our manufacturers. We always tend to keep the focus in our customers and emphasize this with all of our employees.

We are investing our best efforts in different programs and training sessions in conjunction with our manufacturers to help provide the best product awareness possible to all our clients.
